German entertainment:

Watching movies in German is a perfect way to have fun whilst learning a language! Listening to people speak German not only widens your vocabulary but helps you improve on your pronunciation! As well as this, German blogs, news articles etc are a great way to build confidence as it facilitates faster learning

  • This movie focuses on A1 content. It is about a Spanish traveller named Nico who loses his bag whilst at the German airport. The language is kept very basic so i recommend this video if you just want a mild taste of German!
  • This movie is part 2 of the video linked above and is about how Nico finds Germany and how he has adapted to it. It's very realistic!
  • This blog consists of a German girl blogging her day-to-day actvities in Germany. She goes into detail about uni life which may be beenficial to those of you who wish to study abroad! It includes English subtitles to!
